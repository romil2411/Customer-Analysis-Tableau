# Customer Analysis Dashboard 📊

## Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce customer behavior and sales performance. By analyzing $233,650,194 in Total Revenue, this dashboard provides actionable insights into regional performance, demographic spending habits, and the effectiveness of discount-driven sales strategies.

## Key Insights from the Data
* Total Revenue: The dataset tracks a total of $233.6M in revenue across various product categories.

* **Regional Market Share:** The South Region is the primary revenue driver, holding 38.37% of the total market share, followed by the Midwest at 26.93%.

* **Sales Seasonality:** A massive peak in revenue occurred in December 2020 ($57.7M), indicating a high sensitivity to holiday season promotions.

* **Target Demographics:** The 30-40 age group represents the highest-spending segment at **$44.8M**, followed closely by the 60-70 ($41.1M) and 20-30 ($39.9M) groups.

* **Category Performance:** Mobiles & Tablets and Appliances are the top-performing categories for both genders, with Appliances alone generating over $15M in revenue from both male and female segments.

* **Promotion Impact:** The Quantity-Discount Correlation scatter plot shows that while high discounts (up to 70%) are utilized, the majority of high-volume orders occur at lower discount tiers, suggesting brand strength over price-cutting.
