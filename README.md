# Customer Analysis Dashboard 📊

## Project Overview
This Tableau project provides a deep dive into customer purchasing behavior across various demographics and regions. By analyzing over **$203M in Total Revenue**, this dashboard identifies high-growth regions, key age demographics, and the relationship between discounts and order quantities to drive data-informed marketing strategies.

## 🚀 Live Interactive Dashboard
[**View Live Dashboard on Tableau Public**](https://public.tableau.com/views/CustomerAnalysis_17720857024010/Dashboard1?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)

## Key Insights from the Data
* **Regional Dominance:** The **South Region** leads the market, contributing to **38.52%** of total revenue share.
* **Peak Seasonality:** A significant revenue spike occurred in **December 2020 ($48.3M)**, likely driven by holiday sales, followed by a secondary peak in April 2021.
* **Target Demographics:** The **30-40 age group** is the highest spending bracket ($39.1M), followed closely by the 60-70 and 40-50 groups.
* **Gender Parity in Spending:** Revenue is nearly balanced between genders, with **Females ($66.27M)** and **Males ($63.85M)** showing similar interests in top categories like Mobiles & Tablets.
* **Discount Strategy:** The Quantity-Discount Correlation suggests that while high discounts are offered, they do not always result in significantly higher order quantities, suggesting room for price optimization.
